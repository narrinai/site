Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 19, 2024
This Privacy Policy explains how Narrin AI collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our AI character chat platform and related services.

This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") outlines how Narrin AI ("we," "our," or "us") handles information collected from users of our artificial intelligence character interaction platform.

This Policy applies to our website and all digital services that link to this Policy (collectively, the "Platform"). By accessing or using our Platform, you agree to the data practices described herein.

If you disagree with any part of this Policy, please discontinue use of our Platform immediately.

1. Information Collection

We gather various types of information to provide and enhance our Platform services. The specific data we collect depends on how you interact with our Platform.

Direct Information Submission

You may voluntarily provide information when you:

  • Create an account or user profile
  • Engage in conversations with AI characters
  • Subscribe to our services or newsletters
  • Contact our support team
  • Participate in surveys or promotional activities

This information may include:

  • Email address and username
  • Payment and billing information
  • Chat conversations and character interactions
  • User-generated content and character creations
  • Communication records with our team

Automated Data Collection

Our Platform automatically collects certain technical information through cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies:

  • Device and browser specifications
  • Internet service provider details
  • Website navigation patterns
  • Session duration and frequency
  • IP address and general location data
  • Platform version and feature usage

Third-Party Sources

We may receive information from external sources, including:

  • Social media platforms when you connect your accounts
  • Referral partners and affiliate networks
  • Analytics and advertising service providers
  • Payment processors and financial institutions

2. Information Usage

We utilize collected information for the following purposes:

  • Platform Operation: Delivering core AI character interaction services
  • Personalization: Customizing user experience and character responses
  • Communication: Sending updates, notifications, and support responses
  • Enhancement: Improving AI models and Platform functionality
  • Security: Preventing fraud and maintaining Platform safety
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns and user preferences
  • Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our terms
  • Marketing: Promoting relevant features and services

We may combine information from different sources to better understand user needs and improve our services. Aggregated and anonymized data may be used for research and development purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in specific circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party vendors who assist with:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Payment processing and billing
  • Customer support and communication
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Marketing and advertising services

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Respond to legal processes and government requests
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety and Platform security
  • Investigate potential violations of our terms

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

User-Directed Sharing

Certain Platform features allow you to share content publicly or with other users, such as creating public AI characters or participating in community features.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and employee training programs
  • Secure data centers with physical protection measures
  • Incident response procedures for potential breaches

While we strive to protect your information, no system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

5. User Rights and Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Account Management

  • Access and update your profile information
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Manage communication preferences

Privacy Controls

  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Adjust cookie and tracking preferences
  • Control public visibility of your content
  • Request correction of inaccurate information

Regional Rights

Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under local privacy laws, including the right to object to certain processing activities or request data portability.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Policy.

Specific retention periods depend on:

  • The type of information and its purpose
  • Legal and regulatory requirements
  • Your account status and activity level
  • Business needs and operational requirements

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it according to our data retention schedule.

7. International Data Transfers

Our Platform operates globally, and your information may be processed in countries other than your residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct
  • Other legally recognized transfer mechanisms

8. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will promptly delete such information. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • Email notifications to registered users
  • Prominent notices on our Platform
  • Website notifications and alerts

Your continued use of our Platform after policy updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.
